Government Subsidies for Farmers

Explore various government subsidies designed to support farmers.

Fertilizer Subsidy

Eligibility: All farmers, including small, marginal, tenant, and sharecropper farmers, are eligible for the fertilizer subsidy, provided they purchase from authorized dealers with Aadhaar-based verification.

How to Apply: Visit pmkisan.gov.in to apply online.

Seed Subsidy

Eligibility: All farmers, including small, marginal, tenant, and sharecropper farmers, are eligible for seed subsidies provided they purchase from government-approved dealers.

How to Apply:Please visit government portal of your state. Or Visit here.

Electricity Subsidy

Eligibility: Tenant Farmers & Sharecroppers : Eligible in some states with valid lease agreements.

How to Apply: Apply through the state electricity board or distribution company (DISCOM).

Irrigation Subsidy

Eligibility:This subsidy is mainly offered under schemes like Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) and various state-level irrigation programs.

How to Apply:Visit pmksy.com to apply online

Pesticide and Organic Farming Subsidy

Eligibility: Tenant Farmers & Sharecroppers : Eligible if they provide lease agreements.

How to Apply: Apply Through Agriculture Department : Available at local agriculture offices.

Interest Subvention / KCC Scheme

Eligibility: Joint Liability Groups (JLGs) & Self-Help Groups (SHGs) Can apply for group-based loans. Fishermen & Dairy Farmers are eligible under extended KCC benefits. Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) can avail of group loans for collective farming.

How to Apply: Visit to your nearest/favourable bank and ask for this subsidy.

Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF)

Eligibility: Farmers & Individual Entrepreneurs those engaged in agricultural activities. Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) groups of farmers managing collective infrastructure.

How to Apply: Visit the AIF poratal.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

Eligibility:Farmers with Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Loans, mandatory for farmers taking crop loans. Non-Loanee Farmers can voluntarily enroll in the scheme.

How to Apply: Visit https://pmfby.gov.in/
