Government Schemes for Farmers

Explore various government schemes designed to support farmers.

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi

Eligibility: Farmers with less than 2 hectares of land.

How to Apply: Visit pmkisan.gov.in to apply online.

Kisan Credit Card Scheme

Eligibility: All farmers, including individual and joint borrowers.

How to Apply: Contact your nearest bank or cooperative society.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

Eligibility: Farmers growing notified crops.

How to Apply: Register through the official portal at pmfby.gov.in.

National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture

Eligibility: Farmers practicing sustainable farming methods.

How to Apply: Apply through your state agriculture department.

Agricultural Technology Management Agency (ATMA)

Eligibility: Farmers practicing sustainable farming methods.(All farmers are eligible.)

How to Apply: Farmers can join local Farmer Interest Groups (FIGs) or contact the district ATMA office for participation..

E-NAM (National Agriculture Market)

Eligibility: Farmers practicing sustainable farming methods.

How to Apply: Apply through E-nam Registration portal - E-Nam Registration portal

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maandhan Yojana (PM-KMY)

Eligibility: Farmers aged 18-40 years with cultivable land up to 2 hectares.

How to Apply: Eligible farmers can enroll at Common Service Centers (CSCs) with necessary documents like Aadhaar card and bank account details.

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

Eligibility:Groups of farmers or clusters willing to adopt organic farming practices

How to Apply:Farmers can form clusters and apply through the respective state nodal agencies or agricultural departments.

Farm Mechanization Program

Eligibility:Preference is given to small & marginal farmers, SC/ST farmers, and women farmers.

How to Apply:Visit the official government portal - AgriMachinery.in

Soil Health Card Scheme

Eligibility:All farmers are eligible.

How to Apply:Farmers can approach local agricultural offices or visit the scheme's official website to get their soil tested and obtain the health card.
